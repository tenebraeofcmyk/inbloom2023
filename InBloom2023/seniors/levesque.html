Aaron Levesque
Aaron Levesque is a lifelong Wichita native. Aaron loves learning, receiving his Associate Degree in Digital Media in the Fall of 2016. He then continued his education by enrolling at Wichita State University in 2019. Aaron works well within any team he’s a part of due to his time as an escape room manager. His time as a manager gave him valuable insight into how to work well with coworkers and clients alike. Aaron is ever curious at heart and brings curiosity and wonder into his design process. Outside of the design world, Aaron is a queer, polyamorous, cat dad who role-plays as a photographer, hand letterer, and gaming enthusiast. After graduation, he will be looking for a team of designers to work with that aligns with his design outlook and that he can collaborate and learn from.
