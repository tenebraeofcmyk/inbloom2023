Madaleine Meadows
Madeleine Meadows is a graduating senior at Wichita State University receiving her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design with a minor in Communications. She specializes in illustration, visual and brand identity, layout, and web design. Meadows has always been an advocate for the arts in all forms. She embraces the opportunity to be creative and open-minded while using her skills to solve visual problems and make the world a better, more beautiful place, one design at a time. After graduation she hopes to enter the industry in agency life, where she can work with like-minded creative individuals to improve the world we live in.
