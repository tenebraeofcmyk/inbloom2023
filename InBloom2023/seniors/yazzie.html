Evan Yazzie
Evan Yazzie loves pop-punk art and shares a passion for understanding and implementing different graphic design movements. Most of his art comes from a need to divert expectations and learn rules to break them. He loves chocolate and the number one way to get him to do anything is a good steak and a cherry coke. Evan tends to take after his grandmother, who has a love for art and being spiritual. If Evan had one thing to tell anyone about himself and his life it would be that hope and faith that there is something better is out there waiting.
