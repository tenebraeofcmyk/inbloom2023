Alexis Painter
Alexis Painter was born in a small town named Jerseyville, Illinois. She had no idea what she wanted to do until she was forced to take a graphic design class in her senior year in high school! Then she decided to live up to the family name, Painter! It was destiny. After college, she plans world domination, however, first, she needs to land a graphic design job. Ultimately she would want to create logos and product designs for small businesses. She believes that small businesses are the backbone of America. She has management skills due to her family owning two restaurants. Her leadership skills have led her to be a former president of SkillsUSA at MooreNorman, and a lead designer in WSU’s NASA Suits team, CosmoShox. When she isn’t designing or working you can find her nose-deep in a book, building her rank in video games, or laughing at memes.
