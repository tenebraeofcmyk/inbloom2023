Becca Nason
Becca Nason has always loved art from a young age, which lead her to pursue graphic design at Wichita State University. To her, this was the perfect way to use her love of visual art and storytelling to help people solve real problems and communicate effectively. She is also interested in motion graphics, 3D modeling, illustration, and eventually wants to break into user experience design. When she’s not working on design, Becca also likes to create comics, read, write, or use any other means to float away into her imagination.
